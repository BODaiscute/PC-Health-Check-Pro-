## 🖥️ What Is PC Health Check Pro?

PC-Health-Check-Pro- is a user-friendly tool that helps you check the condition of your computer. ## 🔎 What Can PC Health Check Pro Do?

This tool looks at many parts of your computer, including:

- **CPU (Processor)**: Shows current temperature and speed to avoid overheating.
- **Memory (RAM)**: Checks how much is used and if it runs properly.
- **Storage (Hard Drive or SSD)**: Finds issues and shows how much space is left.
- **Battery (on laptops)**: Reports health and charging status.
- **System Stability**: Detects errors or settings that could cause crashes.
- **Network**: Ensures your internet connection is stable.

PC-Health-Check-Pro- helps you understand your system and take steps to keep it healthy.

## 📋 How to Use PC Health Check Pro

### Starting a Health Check

1. Open the app by double-clicking its icon.
2. Click the big **"Scan My PC"** button on the main screen.
3. Wait a few moments as the tool checks your system.

### Reading the Results

- **Green** indicators mean things are working well.
- **Yellow** means some parts could use attention.
- **Red** flags serious issues that need fixing.

The tool uses simple terms, like "CPU temperature is normal" or "Your hard drive has errors."

### Taking Action

PC Health Check Pro offers suggestions to improve performance or fix problems, such as:

- Closing unused programs
- Cleaning up disk space
- Updating drivers or software
- Checking for overheating and improving cooling

Follow the advice shown in the app to keep your computer healthy.
